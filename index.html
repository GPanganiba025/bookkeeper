About Me

Numbers are my language.

Hi, I'm Gerald Panganiban — a certified QuickBooks Bookkeeper with 6+ years of experience in full-cycle accounting and virtual bookkeeping. I help businesses maintain accurate financial records, streamline processes, and gain clear financial insights to support confident decision-making.


My expertise includes:

  • ✅ QuickBooks Online (certified)
  • ✅ Bank and credit card reconciliations
  • ✅ Accounts Payable & Receivable management
  • ✅ Journal entries, month-end close, and clean-ups
  • ✅ Financial reporting: Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet

I've successfully supported U.S. and international clients in industries such as real estate, energy, and technology — managing everything from high-volume transaction reconciliations to project-based clean-ups. I prioritize clear communication, accuracy, and efficiency, making complex financial data easy to understand for business owners.


If you're looking for a reliable bookkeeper who can keep your books clean and your workflow efficient, let's connect.

What I offer

Services

Professional bookkeeping services tailored to your business needs.

Bookkeeping with QuickBooks Online

Full-cycle bookkeeping including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and payroll postings. Bank and credit card reconciliations, transaction categorization, journal entries, financial reporting (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow), and month-end close.

Data Entry & Workflow Management

Importing and organizing data into QuickBooks, Excel, or Google Sheets. Creating rules to streamline recurring transactions, accurate recording of vendor bills, receipts, and invoices, and maintaining clean records for compliance and reporting.

Financial Research & Analysis

Reviewing records for discrepancies and correcting errors, identifying trends in expenses and revenue, supporting cash flow and profitability analysis for decision-making, and preparing data for tax filings and audits.

Client Communication & Support

Providing clear financial updates to clients and management teams, collaborating on financial strategy and performance insights, answering queries, assisting with financial workflows, and ensuring transparency in all communications.
